Usage:
  draft2keys <draft-file>

Citation forms parsed:
  - LaTeX commands \cite...{key1,key2} with optional * and up to two bracket arguments
  - Pandoc inline citations @key and [@key]

Output:
  - Unique keys in first-occurrence order, one per line

Exit codes:
  - 0 at least one key printed
  - 1 no citations found
  - 2 usage or configuration error (e.g. missing draft, missing rg)

Examples:
  draft2keys draft.md > keys.txt
  key=$(sed -n '1p' keys.txt)
  cite2md --cat "$key"
