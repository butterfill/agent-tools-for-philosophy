find-bib — search a CSL-JSON bibliography for matching entries

Usage:
  find-bib [filters...] [--cat]

Filters (repeatable; case-insensitive substrings):
  --author TXT
  --year YYYY
  --doi TXT
  --title TXT
  --abstract TXT

Output:
  - default: print matching keys (one per line)
  - --cat: print each match as BibTeX via cite2bib

Exit codes:
  - 0 at least one match emitted
  - 1 no matches
  - 2 usage or configuration error (e.g. missing jq, bad flag)

Examples (list → then read one):
  find-bib --author vesper --year 2013 --title jump > keys.txt
  key=$(sed -n '1p' keys.txt)
  cite2bib "$key"
  # Or: p=$(cite2md "$key")

Notes:
  - Supports multiple filters of the same type to narrow long lists.
  - Pair with cite2md or cite2pdf to inspect fulltext once a key is selected.
