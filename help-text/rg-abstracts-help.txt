rg-abstracts — run ripgrep against Markdown abstracts

Usage:
  rg-abstracts [rg-flags...] [--] [pattern] [paths...]

Behavior:
  - Restricts searches to Markdown files located under ABSTRACTS_DIR
  - Rejects absolute paths and parent-directory references in positional arguments
  - Blocks ripgrep type/glob overrides (-t/-T/--glob/--iglob/--type-*)

Exit codes:
  - Mirrors ripgrep’s status (0 match found, 1 no matches, >1 error)

Examples (discovery-only triage):
  rg-abstracts -l -i "joint action"
  rg-abstracts -i -C2 "motor representation"

Notes:
  - Abstracts are for finding candidate sources only.
  - Do not use abstracts to fact-check drafts or report what papers argue.
